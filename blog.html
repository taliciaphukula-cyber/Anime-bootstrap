🎌 Anime & Coding

Building My First Anime Website

📅 February 9, 2026

Today was an exciting day! I built my first multi-page website dedicated to anime. Inspired by sites like MyAnimeList, I wanted to create a space where fellow fans could explore and share their love for anime. Using HTML and CSS, I structured pages for home, about, projects, gallery, blog, and contact. The best part? Adding anime-themed colors and gradients that remind me of my favorite series!

🎨 CSS & Design

CSS Styling: Making It Look Like Anime

📅 February 10, 2026

CSS is like the animation studio that brings the story to life! Today I learned how to use gradients, shadows, and hover effects to make my website pop. I created a color scheme inspired by popular anime - purples and pinks like from "Demon Slayer" and blues like from "One Piece". The progress bars on my about page now look like power levels from "Dragon Ball Z"!
